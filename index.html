This is a weekly blog about human exploration of space. Human space flight has been confined to the Earth and the Moon since the 1960's but recent developments in technology look set to push the boundaries of where people can travel to. Humans landing on Mars, once seen as science fiction, could soon be a reality in many of our lifetimes. Human exploration of the solar system and beyond may not be as far away as we think.


Each week at Into The Cosmos, we will explore a different topic about human spaceflight and the exploration of our solar system. We will also look at the astounding advances in exploring space through unmanned vehicles and long distance observation.
